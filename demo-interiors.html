Process

The Resin Art Process

Premium food-safe, UV-resistant epoxy resin is hand-mixed with pigments, poured in multiple controlled layers, and cured at precise temperatures. Each layer adds depth and transparency.

Wood Sources

Sustainably Sourced

Every slab comes from certified sustainable sources — reclaimed, fallen, or plantation-grown timber. We partner with FSC-certified suppliers across India and Southeast Asia.

Warranty

10-Year Guarantee

Every piece carries a 10-year structural warranty and a 5-year finish warranty. Our resin formulations are tested to resist yellowing, heat, and impact for decades of beauty.

03 — Wall Panels

Walls That Speak

Transform plain walls into architectural statements. From fluted timber to geometric 3D panels, each design adds texture, depth, and personality to any interior.

Fluted Wood Panels

Trending

Vertical ribbed profiles in solid teak or oak. Creates elongated visual height with refined linear rhythm. Available in 19mm, 25mm, and 38mm groove widths.

Geometric 3D Panels

Signature

CNC-milled MDF or wood with deep geometric reliefs — hexagons, diamonds, waves. Painted or veneered. Creates dramatic shadow play with directional lighting.

Reclaimed Wood Cladding

Eco-Luxury

Authentic aged patina of reclaimed barn wood, railway sleepers, and teak boat planks. Each piece carries its own history. Maximum character with minimum environmental impact.

Leather & Metal Panels

Bespoke

Upholstered leather panels with brass studding, or perforated brushed metal sheets combined with timber. Perfect for boardrooms, home theatres, and hotel lobbies.
